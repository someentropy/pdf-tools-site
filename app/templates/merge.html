How to Merge PDF Files Online

Combining multiple PDF files into a single document helps organize related information and makes sharing easier. CarbonPDF's merge tool lets you combine up to 5 PDF files in just a few clicks, with no signup required.

When to Merge PDF Files

  • Combining chapters of a report or book
  • Creating a single document from scanned pages
  • Assembling application forms and supporting documents
  • Consolidating receipts or invoices
  • Creating comprehensive presentation materials

How Our PDF Merger Works

Our PDF merger tool is designed for simplicity and efficiency:

  1. Upload up to 5 PDF files in your desired order
  2. Click the "Merge" button
  3. Download your combined PDF file

The files will be merged in the exact order you upload them, preserving all text, images, and formatting from the original documents.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I rearrange pages after merging?
Our current tool merges files in the order they're uploaded. For page rearrangement, you might need a PDF editor after merging.
Do merged PDFs maintain bookmarks and links?
Yes, our merge tool preserves internal links, bookmarks, and other interactive elements from the original files.
